# IdeaForge
HACKETHON




AI + Data-Driven Entrepreneurial Trust Platform**

### **1️⃣ Problem**

* Aspiring entrepreneurs in India face **low trust**, lack of mentorship, and limited access to funding.
* Many **innovative ideas fail early** due to no structured ecosystem for validation and credibility.

---

### **2️⃣ Solution**

A **platform that builds trust using AI and data**:

1. **AI Idea Validator** – Scores ideas based on market trends, similar ventures, and feasibility.
2. **Mentorship Matching** – Connects entrepreneurs with the right mentors, investors, or incubators.
3. **Data Ecosystem of Trust** – Securely tracks projects, milestones, and progress for verifiable credibility.
4. **Optional:** Reputation system – “LinkedIn for Startups” to showcase verified achievements.

---

### **3️⃣ Tech Pillars**

* **AI/ML:** Idea scoring, mentor matching, recommendation system
* **Data Layer:** Cloud database, secure storage, dashboards
* **Frontend:** Web/mobile interface for submissions, tracking, and analytics
* **Optional:** Blockchain for milestone verification

---

### **4️⃣ Impact**

* Builds **trust in entrepreneurs**, making it easier to get funding and mentorship.
* Encourages innovation and reduces early startup failures.
* Provides investors and banks **transparent, verifiable proof** of traction.
* Supports national initiatives: **Startup India & Digital India**.

---

### **5️⃣ MVP Scope for Hackathon**

* Idea submission + AI scoring
* Mentor matching prototype
* Progress tracking dashboard
